Difficult Intubation App

Transfer Difficult Intubation Information Electronically
  • This website is for medical care providers to generate a difficult intubation letter
  • Patients will have the option to easily store the information electronically on their smartphone with a downloadable free app.
  • Patients keep and store their own data.
  • This important information may be shown, printed, transferred to subsequent care providers.
  • No patient information is stored.
